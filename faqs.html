Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are your operating hours?
A: We are open Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM.

Q: Do you accept insurance?
A: Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact us for specifics.

Q: How do I book an appointment?
A: You can book an appointment by calling us or using the online booking form on our website.

Q: Do you offer same-day appointments?
A: Yes, we accommodate same-day appointments for urgent needs when possible.

Q: Do you offer telemedicine services?
A: Yes, we offer telemedicine for consultations if you are unable to visit in person.

Q: What should I bring to my first appointment?
A: Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card, and a list of any medications you are currently taking.

Q: Can I get a prescription refilled online?
A: Yes, prescriptions can be refilled through our online portal or by contacting our office directly.

Q: Do you offer pediatric care?
A: Yes, we provide comprehensive pediatric care, including vaccinations, check-ups, and specialized treatments for children.
